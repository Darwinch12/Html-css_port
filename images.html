An inspiring moment at the sunset

This image reflects exactly the predominant feeling that was surrounding that place, calmness and tenderness. Being able to capture such charming expression of nature was truly rewarding and it brought me a special moment of peace of mind.

The way that the sunlight is reflected in the river and as the darkened branches draws a border to it emphasizes its simplicity, there is not much of details in it yet it matches perfectly well.
